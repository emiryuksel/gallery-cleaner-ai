# SwipeBox

**Your photos deserve better than clutter.**

SwipeBox is a gesture-driven photo gallery cleaner designed exclusively for iPhone. Swipe right to keep. Swipe left to clean. No complexity — just motion.

---

## How It Works

1. **Swipe** through your photo library with fluid, physics-based gestures
2. **Review** your selections on a frosted-glass interface
3. **Clean** — reclaim storage in a single tap

That's it. No accounts required. No cloud uploads. Everything stays on your device.

---

## Design

Built with a glassmorphism design system that feels native to iOS — depth, luminance, and translucency working together to keep the focus on your photos.

- Animated directional feedback on every swipe
- Smooth 60fps transitions powered by Reanimated
- Haptic responses that feel intentional
